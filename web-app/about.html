we simplify the solution for your car care needs. Our company is structured to provide quality, inexpensive, hassle-free car wash and detail services conveniently at your facility.

WHY CHOOSE US

All of our services are perform by hand, we utilized little to no water for our service. We used only about 1 to 2 gallons of clear water depend on the condition of the vehicle. By the time we are done with the vehicle, the water that drip on to the ground are minimal in amount and will be evaporated. There will be no running water on the facility, we have never encounter any complaint from our clients in this field. All the chemical that we use is an environmental friendly product from Auto Magic and 3D Production. We apply the chemical on a towel or a sponge before apply it to the body of the vehicle. There will not be any chemical dispose onto the ground. We take extreme caution toward waste management and disposal, so you can be at ease that we will always leave your facility in the same condition we found it..
